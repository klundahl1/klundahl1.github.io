A Healthy Body

Fitness

  1. A happy mind will only thrive if the body is happy as well. Giving it the natural endorphins by exerting yourself in physical fitness releases chemicals that make you feel happier.
  2. Exercising increases the flow of oxygen and nutrients to the body which leaves the cardiovascular system working less and overall health.
  3. Our bodies were designed for all types of physical exertion. Exercising the body by a variety of sports, going for a walk, even something like hulahooping or jump roping moves the body.
  4. Finding what moves you to move is important because everyone has a different relationship with their body and therefore different activities inspire/engage the mind as well.
  5. The right mindset will make it a positive experience and will help make it more fun for more exercise in the future. Once the mind sees it as a chore, the body will act accordingly and exercise will be put on the backburner.

Eating natural and unprocessed foods

  1. Flavoring food without salt, eat less sugar, because what you put in you get out.
  2. More greens, grains, protein.
.

Drink Water!

  1. Our body is between 50-65% water, we need to keep it replenished
  2. Many of the issues in health come from dehydration.
  3. Water is the answer to most external body pains. Headache? Drink water. Hungry? Drink water.
  4. Many times our body confuses hunger with thirst because many foods naturally have amounts of water in them and hunger sometimes is our body telling us we just need a source of water.

Breathe

  1. Shallow breaths taken are in response to stressful emotions.
  2. We tend to eat too quickly and talk too much.
  3. By taking deep breaths we are encouraged to be silenced and listen to what is happening around us. This helps ground us, and link our outer experience with our inner experience.
  4. Listening to others, we are able to quietly form our own opinions based off of theirs. We are all resources of knowledge for each other. Have the patience to learn.
